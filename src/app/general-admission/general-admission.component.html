Camel Rides

Open all day from Memorial Day weekend - Labor Day weekend
*Weather permitting

  • For ages 2 years and older.
  • More than one person may ride with a weight limit of 250 lbs.
  • Helmet not provided.
  • $5 per rider

Pony and Donkey Rides

Open all day from Memorial Day weekend - Labor Day weekend
*Weather permitting

  • For ages 2 years and older with a weight limit of 80 lbs.
  • Younger riders may require a parent to walk along side the pony.
  • Helmet not provided.
  • $5 per rider

Wagon Rides

Open all day from Labor Day weekend - the Sunday after Halloween
*Weather permitting

  • Autumn is park owners, Big Joel and Mimi's favorite time of the year!
  • They love to decorate for the fall season and especially for Halloween, to transform Big Joel's Safari into Big Joel's Spooky Safari!
  • The wagon tour goes through the park's large field, which is home to:
    • Deer
    • Elk
    • Bison
    • Watusi cattle
    • As well as the many ducks and geese
  • During the tour, the ride operator will provide facts about the animal residents and the history of Big Joel's Safari!
  • $2.50 per rider
