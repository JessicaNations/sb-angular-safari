Best age to visit?

  • Animal lovers of all ages are welcome.

Which animals can be touched and fed?

  • Almost all animals that can walk up to the fence are approachable, unless signs say otherwise.
  • Some love to be touched, but cannot be fed.
  • Some love to be fed, but not touched.
  • The animals can be fed by hand, spoon, or special feed tubes.
  • Feeding directly from the feed cup is discouraged.
  • Hand washing stations are near the front of the park.

Are reservations required for large groups?

  • School tours and field trips must be scheduled in advance.
  • Call: (636) 745-3031 to schedule a group visit.
