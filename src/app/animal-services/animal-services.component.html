Live Nativities and Mobile Animal Services!

  • Upon request, a nativity could include a variety of barn animals, such as:
    • Camels
    • Donkeys
    • Goats
    • Sheep
    • A Small Cow
    • And Sometimes Poultry

Mobile animal services are also available for:

Animal Appearances and Rentals:
