Birthday Parties

Looking for a cool and fun place to have a birthday party?
Big Joel's Safari is the place for a truly unique experience!

What's included in the Safari Birthday Package

  • Admission for 10 kids and 10 adults.
  • Additional guests must pay the standard park entrance fee.
  • Free admission for the birthday child's guardians and up to four additional family members.
  • Each kid will recieve a small souvenir feed cup with animal feed for the animals.
  • Party guests have private access to the Safari Party Room for 2 hours, with 30 additional minutes for prep-time beforehand.
  • The party room has Safari themed decorations and is climate controlled.
  • Basic cups, plates, napkins, and utensils are provided.

Party Rules

Guests are encouraged to go over the park's policies before their event.

  • All guests must check-in at the front desk before entering the park.
  • Guests are permitted to bring food and drink into the party room.
  • Alchohol is prohibited.
  • All food and drink must stay in the party room for the safety of the animals.
  • Guests are permitted to bring balloons and other party decorations.
  • Balloons and party favors, such as whistles are to stay in the party room.

Venue Rental Pricing

$100 deposit is required for all event reservations.
